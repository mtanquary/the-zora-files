# Planning — guide

This folder is for strategic planning, research, and scouting that doesn't belong in a specific episode folder yet.

## What goes here

- **Location scouting** — research on potential future locations, driving distances, trail info, sunrise angles
- **Season planning** — episode arcs, themes, and sequencing for a full season
- **Gear research** — comparison notes when evaluating equipment purchases
- **Content strategy** — audience research, keyword research, competitive analysis
- **Partnership/sponsor notes** — potential collaborations, outreach tracking

## What does NOT go here

- Episode-specific planning → goes in `episodes/season-XX/`
- Production workflow docs → goes in `production/`
- Brand assets → goes in `brand/`
- Task tracking → lives in `PLAN.md` at the project root
